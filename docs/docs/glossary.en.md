# Glossary

> Explanation of Dutch financial and domain-specific terms you'll encounter in myAdmin.

## Dutch → English

| Term                                | English translation           | Explanation                                                           |
| ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Aangifte**                        | Declaration / Tax return      | An official filing with the Dutch tax authority                       |
| **Aangifte IB**                     | Income tax return             | Annual income tax declaration                                         |
| **Afschrift**                       | Bank statement                | Overview of transactions from your bank account                       |
| **Balans**                          | Balance sheet                 | Overview of assets and liabilities at a point in time                 |
| **Belastingdienst**                 | Tax authority                 | The Dutch government tax agency                                       |
| **BNB**                             | BNB (Bed and Breakfast)       | Short-term rental accommodation                                       |
| **Boekjaar**                        | Fiscal year                   | The period for your administration (usually Jan 1 - Dec 31)           |
| **BTW**                             | VAT (Value Added Tax)         | Dutch sales tax on goods and services                                 |
| **Crediteur**                       | Creditor                      | Party you owe money to (supplier)                                     |
| **Crediteurenbeheer**               | Accounts payable              | Management of outstanding debts to suppliers                          |
| **Creditnota**                      | Credit note                   | Document that corrects a previously sent invoice (partially or fully) |
| **Debiteur**                        | Debtor                        | Party that owes you money (customer)                                  |
| **Debiteurenbeheer**                | Accounts receivable           | Management of outstanding receivables from customers                  |
| **Duplicaat**                       | Duplicate                     | A transaction that was already imported previously                    |
| **Factuur**                         | Invoice                       | Bill for delivered goods or services                                  |
| **Grootboekrekening**               | General ledger account        | Account in the bookkeeping for categorizing transactions              |
| **Huurinkomsten**                   | Rental income                 | Income from renting out property                                      |
| **Inkomstenbelasting (IB)**         | Income tax                    | Tax on your income                                                    |
| **Kwartaal**                        | Quarter                       | Three-month period (Q1-Q4)                                            |
| **KvK-nummer**                      | Chamber of Commerce number    | Unique registration number at the Chamber of Commerce                 |
| **Mutatie**                         | Transaction / Mutation        | A financial posting or change                                         |
| **Mutaties**                        | Transactions                  | Plural of mutatie — all financial postings                            |
| **Omzet**                           | Revenue / Turnover            | Total income from business activities                                 |
| **Omzetbelasting**                  | Sales tax / VAT               | Synonym for BTW                                                       |
| **Patroon**                         | Pattern                       | Rule for automatic account assignment to transactions                 |
| **Periode**                         | Period                        | Time frame for reporting (month, quarter, year)                       |
| **STR**                             | STR (Short-Term Rental)       | Short-term rental via platforms like Airbnb                           |
| **Tenant**                          | Tenant                        | An organization or user group within myAdmin                          |
| **Toeristenbelasting**              | Tourist tax                   | Municipal tax on overnight stays                                      |
| **Urenregistratie**                 | Time tracking                 | Tracking hours worked per client and project                          |
| **Verouderingsanalyse**             | Aging analysis                | Overview of outstanding invoices per age category                     |
| **Winst- en verliesrekening (W&V)** | Profit & Loss statement (P&L) | Overview of income and expenses over a period                         |
| **ZZP**                             | Self-employed (no employees)  | Zelfstandige Zonder Personeel — freelancer                            |
