# Quick Entry (Mobile)

> Register trips from your phone with minimal steps.

## Overview

Quick Entry is a mobile-optimized version of the trip registration. You open it via your phone browser or as an app icon on your home screen. Ideal for on the go: register a trip in a few taps without navigating through menus.

!!! info
Quick Entry uses the same app and the same login credentials as the desktop version. Your data is synchronized immediately.

## What you need

- A smartphone with internet connection
- Your myAdmin login credentials
- At least one registered vehicle

## Opening

Quick Entry is accessible at:

```
https://[your-domain]/zzp/ritten/quick
```

1. Open your phone browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox)
2. Navigate to the above address
3. Log in with your regular credentials
4. Quick Entry appears as a full-screen form

## Add to home screen

### iOS (iPhone/iPad)

1. Open Safari and go to the Quick Entry URL
2. Log in so your session is active
3. Tap the **Share icon** (square with arrow up) at the bottom of the screen
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
5. Give the shortcut a name (e.g., "Trips") and tap **Add**
6. The icon appears on your home screen

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open Chrome and go to the Quick Entry URL
2. Log in so your session is active
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top right
4. Tap **Add to Home screen**
5. Give the shortcut a name (e.g., "Trips") and tap **Add**
6. The icon appears on your home screen

## Route presets

On the Quick Entry screen your favorite routes are shown as large, tappable cards:

1. View the cards with your frequently used routes
2. **Tap on a route card** to select the route
3. The start address, end address, category and trip purpose are filled automatically
4. Only enter the **end odometer**
5. Tap **Save**

!!! tip
Presets are automatically learned based on your most driven routes. You can also manage them manually via the desktop version.

## Odometer

The start odometer is automatically filled with the end reading of your previous trip:

| Situation                           | What happens                                    |
| ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Previous trip end reading available | Start odometer is automatically filled          |
| No previous trip (first time)       | Fill in the start odometer manually             |
| Odometer differs from dashboard     | Adjust the start odometer (gap-fill is created) |

## Start/Stop workflow

For trips you want to register in real-time (you get in the car and register immediately):

### Start a trip

1. Open Quick Entry
2. Select a route preset or fill in the start address
3. Tap **Start trip**
4. The system records the start time
5. The trip is now "open" — you can start driving

### Stop a trip

1. Open Quick Entry (your open trip is shown)
2. Tap **Stop trip**
3. The system records the end time
4. Fill in the **end address** (if you didn't use a preset)
5. Fill in the **end odometer**
6. Tap **Save**

!!! info
You don't have to use the Start/Stop workflow. You can also fill in all details at once after your trip.

## Tips for daily use

### Recommendations

- **Register immediately** — do it before or after each trip, not retroactively at the end of the week
- **Use presets** — after a few weeks you'll have your regular routes as quick selections
- **Home screen icon** — save Quick Entry as an app icon so it's always at hand
- **End odometer** — read it as soon as you turn off the engine, before you forget
- **One vehicle** — if you only have one car, it is automatically selected

### When to use the desktop version?

Quick Entry is ideal for registering individual trips. Use the full desktop version for:

- Viewing overviews and reports
- Filtering and exporting trips
- Managing route presets manually
- Adding or editing vehicles
- Importing historical trips
- Creating invoices from trips

## Troubleshooting

| Problem                          | Cause                              | Solution                                       |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Quick Entry doesn't load         | No internet connection             | Check your WiFi or mobile data                 |
| Session expired (login again)    | Inactive for too long              | Log in again — your session will be renewed    |
| Home screen icon no longer works | URL changed or cache cleared       | Remove the icon and add it again               |
| Route presets don't appear       | Too few trips or cache not loaded  | Wait for more trip history or refresh the page |
| Vehicle not shown                | Vehicle is deactivated             | Reactivate the vehicle via the desktop version |
| End odometer cannot be saved     | Value is lower than start odometer | Check if you entered the correct reading       |
