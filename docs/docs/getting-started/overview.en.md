# What is myAdmin?

> A brief overview of the platform and what you can do with it.

## Overview

myAdmin is a financial management platform built for processing bank transactions, invoices, short-term rentals, and tax declarations. It combines manual control with smart automation so you spend less time on administration.

## What can you do with it?

### Process banking transactions

Import CSV bank statements (Rabobank format), let the system automatically assign accounts based on patterns, and filter out duplicates.

### Manage invoices

Upload PDF invoices and let AI extract the details. Review the results, adjust where needed, and store everything in Google Drive.

### Short-term rental (STR)

Process revenue files from Airbnb and Booking.com. View realized and planned bookings and future revenue summaries.

### Smart pricing recommendations

Receive AI-powered pricing recommendations for your rental properties, including event-based price adjustments.

### Reports and dashboards

View interactive dashboards, generate profit & loss statements and balance sheets, and export to Excel.

### Prepare tax declarations

Prepare VAT (BTW), income tax (IB), and tourist tax declarations using the correct data from your administration.

### Validate PDF links

Check whether Google Drive links in your transactions still work and fix broken links.

## Who is myAdmin for?

- **Property managers** managing multiple rental properties
- **Financial staff** processing bank transactions and invoices
- **Administrators** managing the platform for multiple tenants

## How is it built?

myAdmin is a web application consisting of:

- A **frontend** (what you see in the browser)
- A **backend** (the server that processes everything)
- A **database** (where your data is stored)

You don't need to install anything — just open your browser and log in.

!!! tip
Use **Test mode** to explore the platform without touching real data. See [Test vs Production](test-vs-production.md) for more information.
