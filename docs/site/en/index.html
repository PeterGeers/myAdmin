    Welcome to myAdmin¶

    Complete documentation for myAdmin financial management.

    What is myAdmin?¶

    myAdmin is an all-in-one financial management platform. It helps you process bank transactions, invoices, short-term rental (STR) operations, and tax declarations — all from a single place.

    Modules¶

    • Banking

    Import bank statements, review transactions, and let patterns automatically assign accounts.

    Upload invoices, let AI extract the details, and manage everything via Google Drive.

    Process bookings from Airbnb and Booking.com, view realized and planned revenue.
